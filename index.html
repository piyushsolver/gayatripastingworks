About Us

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we've built a reputation for reliability and quality in the packaging industry.

Gayatri Pasting Works is a trusted name in the packaging industry, providing high-quality packaging solutions to businesses of all sizes. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for many leading brands.

Our state-of-the-art facilities in Mahape and Ambernath are equipped with modern machinery to produce a wide range of packaging products that meet the diverse needs of our clients across various industries.

We take pride in our attention to detail, timely delivery, and customer-centric approach that has helped us build long-term relationships with some of the biggest names in the food, retail, and pharmaceutical industries.
